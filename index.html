Startups 4 Corona

Startups 4 Corona is a community of entrepreneurs / startups / businesses with a common goal of developing solutions to fight against Corona virus and Covid-19

Let's fight the virus together

The COVID-19 drove the entire world into a state of turmoil. Presently, dispersion of the pandemic is at a brisk pace, which is quite alarming. However, shielding from the affect of the Corona Virus is absolutely possible through adopting the precautionary measures. Evidently, Governments of all the nations are functioning to facilitate the triumph over the chaotic situation.

Nevertheless, the cooperation from the citizens’ side is decidedly anticipated in this regard. Individual decontamination, societal detachment (of course temporarily), usage of face masks, sanitizers, hand gloves etc. while on move, are the essential inclinations to be espoused so as to thwart the Corona Virus.

Read More

As the situation is global, scarcity of the protective commodities (i.e. face masks, sanitizers, hand gloves head shields etc.) prevails to a large extent. At this juncture, Indian Startups intend to combat against the Corona Virus. A task force is shaped up under the aegis of Indian Startups with an objective of generating entrepreneurs across the globe to encounter the Corona Virus. So as to conquer the cited scarcity, the Corona Virus Startups Task Force contemplates on launching the startups in enormous numbers, that manufacture the safety commodities to meet the need of the hour.

At the moment Indian Startups aid the entrepreneurs in contriving face masks, sanitizers, hand gloves, head shields, safety accessories and allied products. The associates of the task force are projected to execute certain chores specifically, coordinating with entrepreneurs in launching the manufacturing units producing safety commodities, maneuvering the trade sketch and assisting the aspirants in this regard, endowing the entrepreneurs with the supply chain logistics, interconnecting the merchants of the raw materials and the entrepreneurs.

Into the bargain, the task force associates need to coordinate with various chapters of Indian Startups, facilitating the progression of the events and sessions allied with the Startups Against Corona Virus. In addition, the associates are anticipated to administer the routine tasks of manufacturing. Systematizing the supply chain and distribution is one of the core functions of the task force associates.

Provision of expert services precisely, medical, legal, patenting, marketing, content development, Web development, IT solutions etc. are expected from the task force associates and we highly appreciate the same. The associates with the proficiency in Quality Assurance, Business Strategy, Personnel Management, Financial Management etc. are anticipated to partake in the Task Force.
